🎯 YOUR MISSION:

  You are a Community Leader during flood season.
  
  OBJECTIVE: Keep Safety ≥ 70% AND Infrastructure ≥ 60%

  Each round:
  1. A flood event happens
  2. You choose an action
  3. See the outcome
  4. Learn a flood safety tip
  5. Maybe answer a quiz (+2 bonus points)

  After 8 rounds → See your results → Play again!


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════


💡 QUICK TIPS:

  • SAFETY is most important—prioritize it!
  • You start with 50 Resource Points (RP) to spend
  • Different strategies lead to different outcomes:
    - PREPARE: Invest early (Reinforce Dikes, Awareness)
    - DEFEND: React fast (Evacuate, Establish Shelters)
    - RECOVER: Rebuild well (Repair Roads, Distribute Aid)
  • Answer quizzes correctly for +2 RP
  • Every action reveals real flood safety advice

         - index.html
         - style.css
         - script.js
         - gameData.json

  Issue: Language switch not working
  Fix:   Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)

  Issue: Buttons not responding
  Fix:   Check browser console (F12) for errors
