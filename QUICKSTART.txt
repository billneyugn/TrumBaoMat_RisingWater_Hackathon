🎮 HOW TO PLAY (IN 30 SECONDS):

1. Open "index.html" in your web browser
   → Game loads automatically

2. Read the Help menu (top right) if you're new

3. Switch to Vietnamese if desired (language buttons, top right)

4. Choose an action each round to manage the flood crisis

5. Keep Safety ≥ 70% and Infrastructure ≥ 60% to win

6. After 8 rounds, see your results and play again!

⚡ QUICK TIPS:

• SAFETY is most critical - prioritize it!
• You start with 50 Resource Points (RP) - spend wisely
• Reinforce dikes early for Prepare strategy
• Evacuate quickly when floods hit for Defend strategy
• Repair and distribute aid for Recover strategy
• Answer quizzes correctly to earn +2 RP bonus
• Every action reveals a real flood safety tip

🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING:

Q: Game won't load?
A: Ensure ALL 5 files are in the SAME folder:
   - index.html
   - style.css
   - script.js
   - gameData.json
   - QUICKSTART.txt (optional)

Q: Language switch doesn't work?
A: Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R), then try again

Q: Quiz isn't showing?
A: Quizzes have 30% chance per round. Keep playing!

Q: Can I play offline?
A: YES! No internet needed. Just double-click index.html
