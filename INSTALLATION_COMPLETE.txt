🎮 WHAT'S INSIDE THE GAME:

8 Events (with quizzes & tips):
  1. Heavy Rainfall Warning
  2. Dike Breach Reported
  3. Water Levels Rising Rapidly
  4. Disease Outbreak Risk
  5. Thousands of Evacuees Arriving
  6. Rescue Operation Required
  7. Water Levels Receding
  8. Clear Weather Period

9 Actions (various costs & effects):
  1. Reinforce Dikes (20 RP)
  2. Evacuate Citizens (15 RP)
  3. Distribute Relief Supplies (15 RP)
  4. Repair Roads (18 RP)
  5. Establish Evacuation Centers (25 RP)
  6. Organize Rescue Teams (20 RP)
  7. Community Awareness Campaign (10 RP)
  8. Provide Medical Support (22 RP)
  9. Do Nothing (0 RP - emergency)

Three Strategic Paths:
  • PREPARE: Invest early (Reinforce, Educate)
  • DEFEND: React fast (Evacuate, Shelter, Rescue)
  • RECOVER: Rebuild well (Repair, Distribute, Medical)

🎮 FIRST TIME PLAYING?

1. Start with English (EN) - easier to learn
2. Read the Help menu (top-right corner)
3. Try the PREPARE strategy first:
   - Reinforce Dikes early
   - Use Community Awareness
   - Build up before crisis
4. Then try DEFEND or RECOVER in future games

Typical winning path:
  → Spend 20 RP on Reinforce Dikes (Round 1)
  → Spend 10 RP on Awareness (Round 2)
  → Respond to crisis when needed
  → Use clear weather to repair & recover
  → Finish with Safety 70%+, Infrastructure 60%+
