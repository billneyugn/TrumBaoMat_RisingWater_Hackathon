🎮 THE GAME IN 30 SECONDS:

You're a Community Leader during flood season (8 rounds).

Each round:
  1. A flood-related event happens
  2. You choose an action
  3. See results on 4 meters (Safety, Infrastructure, Morale, RP)
  4. Learn a real flood safety tip
  5. Maybe answer a quiz (+2 bonus points)

After 8 rounds:
  → If Safety ≥ 70% AND Infrastructure ≥ 60% = YOU WIN! 🎉
  → Otherwise = Try again with a different strategy

Playable in ~10 minutes. Replay with different strategies!

🎮 THREE WAYS TO WIN:

STRATEGY 1: PREPARE
  Round 1-2: Reinforce Dikes, Awareness Campaign
  Goal: Build resilience before crisis
  Risk: Moderate (requires forward thinking)

STRATEGY 2: DEFEND
  React quickly to events
  Use: Evacuate, Establish Shelters, Rescue Teams
  Risk: High (expensive emergency responses)

STRATEGY 3: RECOVER
  Focus on rebuilding
  Use: Repair Roads, Distribute Aid, Medical Support
  Risk: Lower (but takes active management)

Best: Mix all three! PREPARE early, DEFEND during crisis, RECOVER after.
